Lately we&#8217;ve been flooded with news regarding malware, ransomware, cryptoware, and other things that we generally called viruses in the past, I&#8217;ll present my theory on how this new wave of cyber flu is just another form of natural selection.

Way back when email was still something of an extravagant form of communication we all told everyone &#8220;don&#8217;t open weird things you don&#8217;t expect from people you don&#8217;t know&#8221; and everything was fine, those who did ended up with annoying things on their computers or at most a credit card detail stealing script.

Nowadays we have antivirus software you can&#8217;t turn off (looking at you Microsoft), software updates every month and every country has a cyber division that supposedly does something to &#8220;fight the cyber threat&#8221;.

The main people who suffered from the WannaCry attacks were people who _**didn&#8217;t update their software**_**** or people who _**were using obsolete software**_.
