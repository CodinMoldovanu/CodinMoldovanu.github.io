While developing a platform I came across the need of a nice CSS framework that would streamline the graphic part of it and rid me of the painstaking task of creating hundreds of divs each with multiple possibile options. I already knew about Bootstrap and having worked with it I tried skipping because I didn&#8217;t think any other framework can&#8217;t do the job. The website I was developing wasn&#8217;t that complicated after all, but it was more than just an About page. Everything from forms to tables et al.

After a quick search I came across Material Design Lite, which is made by Google and respects the Material Design guidelines imposed for the Android OS, but I noticed that it has multiple missing points, so after researching a bit I found MaterializeCSS which is basically the same thing but better with a few more options available.
